About Lore Olympus

Lore Olympus is a modern retelling of the classic Greek myth of Hades and Persephone. The story unfolds in a visually stunning graphic format, blending contemporary themes with ancient mythology. It follows the journey of Persephone, the innocent goddess of spring, as she navigates her new life in the underworld after being abducted by Hades, the enigmatic god of the dead.

This captivating webtoon explores themes of love, power, and identity, delving into the complexities of relationships among gods and mortals alike. With its rich character development, Lore Olympus challenges traditional narratives by portraying the gods as flawed yet relatable beings. The art style, characterized by vibrant colors and expressive illustrations, enhances the emotional depth of the story, drawing readers into a world of magic and intrigue.

As Persephone discovers her own strength and desires, she confronts the challenges posed by her mother, Demeter, and the social dynamics of Olympus. Hades, often misunderstood, reveals his softer side, creating a compelling love story that transcends the boundaries of their realms. With humor, drama, and heartfelt moments, Lore Olympus redefines the myths we thought we knew, making them accessible and engaging for a modern audience.

Main Characters

Hades

King of the Underworld

Hades is often depicted as the brooding and stoic ruler of the Underworld. With a dark and mysterious presence, he commands respect and fear. While he initially comes across as cold and detached, Hades possesses a deep emotional complexity. He is profoundly in love with Persephone.

Persephone

Goddess of Spring and Queen of the Underworld

Persephone is the vibrant goddess of spring and the reluctant queen of the underworld. She is torn between her duty and her desire for freedom. Her relationship with Hades brings her inner strength and determination as she grows into a powerful figure.

Zeus

King of the Gods

Zeus, the ruler of the Olympian gods, is known for his authoritative and charismatic presence. As the most powerful of the gods, he often meddles in the affairs of both mortals and deities. His wisdom and experience guide him, but his impulsive nature.

Hermes

Messenger of the Gods

Hermes is the swift-footed messenger of the gods, known for his wit, intelligence, and agility. He often finds himself in the middle of divine schemes, offering guidance to both gods and mortals alike. Despite his playful nature, Hermes plays a crucial role in ensuring the balance between realms.

Athena

Goddess of Wisdom and War

Athena is the goddess of wisdom and war, revered for her intelligence and strategic prowess. She is a mentor to heroes and a protector of cities. Athena's calm and quite sets her apart from gods, earning her the respect and admiration mortals and deities.
